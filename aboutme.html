About me

I'm Kimberly Vanderbeken and I'm studying "glass and ceramics" in Sint-Lucas campus Ghent,  located in Belgium. Currently I'm in the second bachelor year.

Clay is my preffered material. You can feel the clay and know what it is going to do, how it is going to behave. As with glass, you learn to understand and forsee it's behaviour. These courses are the only ones where the physical connection and feelings between the student and the materials are at its maximum.
That is the reason why I chose for this subject.
