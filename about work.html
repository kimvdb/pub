About work


In my first year I had no clue what I was going to do, which direction I would choose.
I was working on a lot of different projects, all of them at the same time. This had as result that I wasn't going search for details nor going deeper in to research. 

This year it is a total difference. Now I'm making organic shapes which are looking like they are going to burst at any time. I want to awaken the feeling of corporeality and safety. My goal is to make shapes that are as tenced as possible and that you don't have a top- or bottomside, works without flat sides. I'm trying to let lose of all control and let the shapes arise from the material itself. Then I started to add lines. At first sightthe lines where leaning towards a decorative value and this is not my intention. I wanted to add movement into my shapes. Later I started to make cuttings in the shapes that are resulting into "injuries" of the objects. Those injuries are emphasizing the corporeality of the shapes.

After a while I started experimenting with another material. I started using nylon stockings together with clay and also as stand-alone object. This material gives me the opportunity to emphasize the corporeality of shapings. These objects are interpreted as fat cells, cysts or foetuses. I'm trying to make clay objects out of the nylon ones, as well as letting them live next to each other.
