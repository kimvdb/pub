About work


At my first year I had absolutely no clue what I was doing. I was working on a lot of different projects all at the same time with as result that I started everything but I didn’t go deeper into the subject, into the research of my project. 

It’s different this year, I make organic shapes which look like they are going to snap at any time. It is my intention to summon a feeling of corporeality and a form of security, but especially to make shapes that are as tense possible and doesn’t have a up- or downside in other words there have no flat sides. I increasingly try to oppose my own controlling character and just let the shapes arise from the material. Then I started to add lines, at first it was far to decorative which wasn’t my intention at all. I wanted to add movement into my shapes. But I started to experiment with cutting into the shapes, resulting in injuring the objects which emphasize the corporeality of the shapes.

Then I started to work with another material then clay as well, namely with nylon stockings to even more emphasize on the corporeality. I began to make nylon objects which remind me of fat cells. I am now trying to see how far I can go to translate my nylon objects in clay or to just let them exists side by side (nylon and clay objects) as relatives of eachother.
